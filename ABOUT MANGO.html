Mango is my beloved dog

Mango is a dog who was adopted in April 28th, 2018. She was wandering around Suwon area and was about to get slaughtered from one of the hospitals

Luckily, there was a lady who was going through a emotional harship, and she really wanted a dog. It was not a coincidence that they met together.
